[Spaghetti Carbonara]
Ingredients:
- 200g spaghetti
- 100g pancetta, diced
- 2 large eggs
- 50g grated parmesan cheese
- 50g Pecorino Romano cheese
- Salt and freshly ground black pepper
- 1 clove garlic, smashed
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
- Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:
Boil spaghetti until al dente. While pasta cooks, sauté pancetta and garlic in olive oil until crispy. In a bowl, whisk eggs, parmesan, and Pecorino Romano with black pepper. Drain pasta, saving some cooking water. Toss pasta in the pancetta pan, then add egg mixture off-heat. Adjust sauce with pasta water. Serve garnished with parsley.

[Chicken Curry]
Ingredients:

- 2 cloves garlic, minced

- 1 tbsp curry powder
- 1 tbsp ginger, grated
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp cumin

- Salt and pepper

Instructions:

[Beef Stroganoff]
Ingredients:
- 300g beef (sirloin or tenderloin)
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 200ml sour cream
- 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
- 1 tbsp flour
- 1 tbsp butter
- 200ml beef stock
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper
- Fresh parsley
- 1/4 cup white wine (optional)
- 1/2 cup mushrooms, sliced

Instructions:
Brown beef in oil, set aside. Sauté onions and mushrooms, then add garlic. Stir in flour, then add wine and beef stock. Stir in mustard and sour cream. Return beef and simmer until thickened. Garnish with parsley.
